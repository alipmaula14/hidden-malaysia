# Hidden Malaysia — Image Credits

All cover images are stored locally under `public/images/posts/<state>/` and served by Express.
Used for the **BIT21503 Web Development** group project at UTHM (educational use).

**15 of 18** images are authentic photographs of the real locations, sourced from
**Wikimedia Commons** under Creative Commons / public-domain licences (attributed below).
The remaining **3** are generic Unsplash stock photos (free licence) for places that have
no Commons photograph available.
